Introducing the Body Mist Collection
 

The Body Mist Collection offers a fresh and effortless way to experience Byredo’s signature fragrances throughout the summer months.

Available across six signature scents, from Mojave Ghost to Alto Astral, the collection invites a more spontaneous fragrance gesture, designed for generous application, layering, and refreshing the scent throughout the day.

Infused with aloe vera, each formula delicately envelops the skin in fragrance while helping maintain its natural hydration. Wear alone, layer with your fragrance ritual, or reapply throughout the day for an instant refreshing effect.

A new sensorial gesture for summer: refreshing, expressive, and made to be layered.
