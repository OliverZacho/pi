The farm
 
Luis Edgar's journey into coffee began with a genuine passion for the crop and a drive to produce something exceptional. Over the years, he has developed a deep understanding of cultivation and processing. 

Finca La Palma stretches across 9 hectares of prime coffee-growing land. It represents a blend of tradition and modernity, where Luis applies traditional agricultural practices alongside contemporary techniques to maximise yield and quality.
 
Bright and lively, with a natural sweetness. The Pink Bourbon carries an elegant complexity, bringing together notes of Danish berries and rosehip into something elegant.
